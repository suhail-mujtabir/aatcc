Why Join AATCC AUST Student Chapter?

The AATCC AUST Student Chapter offers more than just membership in a professional organization—it provides a transformative platform for students to develop both technical expertise and leadership skills. Members gain access to cutting-edge research in textile chemistry, colorist sciences, and sustainable textile production methods. The chapter regularly organizes workshops, seminars, and industrial visits that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Furthermore, being part of AATCC opens doors to international networking opportunities with professionals and researchers from around the globe. Students can participate in global competitions, present research papers at conferences, and collaborate on projects that address real-world challenges in the textile industry. This exposure is invaluable for those who aspire to become leaders in Bangladesh's textile sector or pursue advanced studies in textile engineering and chemistry.
